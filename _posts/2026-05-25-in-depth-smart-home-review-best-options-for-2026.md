## Buying Guide: How to Choose the Right Smart Home Device

**Ecosystem first.** Before buying any smart home device, decide which voice assistant you want at the centre — Alexa, Google Home, or Apple HomeKit. Mixing ecosystems creates friction. Pick one and buy compatible devices.

**Connectivity type matters.** Wi-Fi devices are easiest to set up. Zigbee devices (like Philips Hue bulbs) are more reliable at scale but need a hub. Z-Wave is common in security systems. If you're starting out, stick to Wi-Fi.

**Subscription costs add up.** Many devices have hidden subscription costs for cloud storage or advanced features (Ring, Wyze, Nest). Factor this into the total cost before buying.

**Expandability.** Buy into platforms that have broad product ranges. A Philips Hue bulb fits into a larger Hue ecosystem. A no-name smart bulb is a dead end.
