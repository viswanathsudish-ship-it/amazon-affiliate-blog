## Buying Guide: How to Choose the Best Tech Accessories

Before you hit that buy button, consider these key factors:

- **Build Quality:** Aluminum and braided cables last longer than plastic.
- **Compatibility:** Check port types (USB-C, USB-A, Lightning) before buying.
- **Power Delivery:** For chargers, ensure wattage matches your device's needs.
- **Portability:** If you travel often, prioritize compact and lightweight options.

Taking a few minutes to evaluate these points can save you from buyer's remorse down the line.
