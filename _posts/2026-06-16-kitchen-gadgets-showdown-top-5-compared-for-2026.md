## Buying Guide: How to Choose the Best Kitchen Gadgets

Before you hit that buy button, consider these key factors:

- **Capacity:** Match the size to your household. A 2-quart air fryer won't feed a family of four.
- **Power:** Higher wattage means faster cooking, but also higher energy use.
- **Ease of Cleaning:** Dishwasher-safe parts save hours of maintenance.
- **Warranty:** A 2+ year warranty signals manufacturer confidence.

Taking a few minutes to evaluate these points can save you from buyer's remorse down the line.
