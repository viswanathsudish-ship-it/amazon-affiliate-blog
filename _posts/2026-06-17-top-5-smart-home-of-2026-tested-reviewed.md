## Buying Guide: How to Choose the Best Smart Home

Before you hit that buy button, consider these key factors:

- **Compatibility:** Ensure the device works with your existing ecosystem (Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit).
- **Connectivity:** Wi-Fi vs Zigbee vs Z-Wave — Wi-Fi is easiest, but mesh protocols are more reliable for large homes.
- **Privacy:** Look for local processing options and clear data policies.
- **Expandability:** Choose brands with broad product lines so you can grow your system.

Taking a few minutes to evaluate these points can save you from buyer's remorse down the line.
